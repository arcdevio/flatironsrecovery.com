About Us

Our Mission

The mission of Flatirons Recovery is to walk alongside our clients through the process of healing from addiction with greater clarity of purpose, connection to community, and ability to take right action in their lives.

Why Us?

What differentiates Flatirons Recovery Center from other treatment providers is our mindfulness-based curriculum, commitment to small group sizes, and belief that all our clients are basically good, and must be treated as such. Our highly skilled, compassionate clinical staff provide outstanding care that meets clients as they are, in order to guide them through the process of healing from a wide range of substance use, and mental health disorders.

Drug and Alcohol Treatment

What Treatment Looks Like at Flatirons Recovery

At Flatirons Recovery, clients can expect compassionate, evidence-based treatment for substance use and dual-diagnosis disorders.

Our treatment team focuses on healing the whole individual, instead of focusing on one specific area of a person’s life that may be problematic. We do this by working with clients to develop action plans for relapse prevention, beginning to heal trauma, learning skills for more successful coping with mental health challenges, eating healthier, getting more consistent exercise, and having fun in recovery.

Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Mental Health

Dual-diagnosis treatment refers to an individual who meets DSM-V diagnostic criteria for both a substance use disorder, as well as a mental health disorder such as, anxitey disorder, bi-polar disorder, and post traumatic stress disorder. These are also referred to as co-occurring disorders.
