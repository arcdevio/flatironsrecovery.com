About Us

Addiction Treatment Center based in Boulder Colorado

Our Mission

The mission of Flatirons Recovery is to walk alongside our clients through the process of healing from drug and alcohol addiction with greater clarity of purpose, connection to community, and ability to take right action in their lives.

Why Us?

What differentiates Flatirons Recovery from other addiction treatment providers is our mindfulness-influenced curriculum, commitment to small group sizes, and belief that all our clients are innately good, and must be treated as such. Our highly skilled, compassionate clinical staff provide outstanding care that meets clients as they are, in order to guide them through the process of healing from a wide range of substance use, and mental health disorders.

Drug and Alcohol Addiction Treatment

What Treatment Looks Like at Flatirons Recovery

At Flatirons Recovery, clients can expect compassionate, evidence-based treatment for substance use and dual-diagnosis disorders.

Our treatment team focuses on healing the whole individual, instead of focusing on one specific area of a person’s life that may be problematic. We do this by working with clients to develop action plans for relapse prevention, beginning to heal trauma, learning skills for more successful coping with mental health challenges, eating healthier, getting more consistent exercise, and having fun in recovery.

Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Mental Health

Dual-diagnosis refers to a person who meets DSM-V diagnostic criteria for both a substance use disorder, as well as a mental health disorder such as depression, anxiety, bi-polar disorder, or post-traumatic stress disorder. Also referred to as co-occurring disorders, the highly skilled therapeutic and medical staff at Flatirons Recovery are trained to address the individual mental health challenges of all our clients.

Learn More - Mental Health

Addiction Treatment

Flatirons Recovery’s addiction treatment program helps clients suffering from a wide range of addictions begin to heal and recover. Our individualized, mindfulness-influenced addictions treatment focuses on building relapse prevention skills, healing trauma and other mental health disorders, developing healthier eating and exercise habits, as well as connecting to supportive communities for recovery.
