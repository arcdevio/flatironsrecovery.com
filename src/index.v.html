About Flatirons Recovery

Our Mission

The mission of Flatirons Recovery Center is to provide client-centered, evidence-based substance use/dual-diagnosis care that takes a holistic approach to treatment.

Why Us?

What differentiates Flatirons Recovery Center from other treatment providers is our commitment to small group sizes, treating clients as whole beings, and the continued development of a compassionate community in which to heal. Our highly skilled, kind, and compassionate clinical staff provide outstanding care that meets clients as they are, in order to guide them through the process of healing from a wide range of substance use, and mental health disorders.

Therapy

Individual

At Flatirons Recovery Center, clients will engage in a weekly, 1-hour individual therapy session in order to develop more insight into the painful habitual patterns that have influenced their substance use disorder, as well as to develop more ability to take appropriate action to begin shifting those.

Group

Along side individual therapy, clients will engage in 30-hours of group programming during PHP/Day Treatment programming, and 9-15 hours of group programming during Intensive Outpatient programming.

Family

At Flatirons Recovery Center, client will engage in a bi-weekly, 1-hour family therapy session, in order to develop more insight into the systemic ways in which substance use disorders may be impacting their family life.

Mental Health

Dual-diagnosis treatment refers to an individual who meets DSM-V diagnostic criteria for both a substance use disorder, as well as a mental health disorder such as, anxitey disorder, bi-polar disorder, and post traumatic stress disorder. These are also referred to as co-occurring disorders.
