Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

treatment and therapy at our addiction treatment center

At Flatirons Recovery, our IOP program meets Monday, Wednesday, and Thursday evenings from 9am - 12pm for group therapy.

Group topics addressed in IOP include, but are not limited to:

  • CBT/DBT skills for relapse prevention
  • Developing mindfulness skills
  • Relationships
  • Role of family in recovery
  • Community-based recovery models such as Smart Recovery and AA/12-Step

Flatirons Recovery’s IOP also includes 1-hour of weekly individual psychotherapy.

Our IOP program lasts anywhere from 4 to 12 weeks to meet the individual treatment needs of different clients.

Clients participating in Flatiron Recovery Center’s IOP program typically live independently at home, or in a nearby sober living facility.
