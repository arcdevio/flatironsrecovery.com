Day Treatment (PHP)

treatment and therapy at our addiction treatment center

Addiction treatment at Flatirons Recovery typically begins in our Day Treatment, or PHP program, which meets Monday through Friday, from 8:30am â€“ 3:00pm. During the week, clients participate in a structured, comprehensive group therapy curriculum that includes, but is not limited to:

  • Foundations for Recovery
  • CBT/DBT skills for relapse prevention
  • Education on the neurobiology of addiction
  • Developing mindfulness skills
  • Nutrition for early recovery
  • Relationships
  • Community-based recovery models such as Smart Recovery and AA/12-Step.
  • Experiential programing including art therapy, equine therapy, hiking, and rock climbing.

Clients may participate in Day Treatment while living at home, though it is recommended that clients live in our staffed sober living house for the full duration of their Day Treatment program. For more information about Flatirons Recovery sober living, please click here.

During Day Treatment, clients will also meet with a therapist for 1-hour of individual therapy per week, participate in family therapy on an as needed basis, as well as meet regularly with our Medical Director for any medication and/or physical health needs. Individual therapy may include biofeedback, art therapy, and trauma therapy such as EMDR, when indicated.

Day Treatment at Flatirons Recovery lasts anywhere from 4 to 12 weeks to meet the individual treatment needs of different clients.

Often, clients transition from Day Treatment to our IOP for 4-12 weeks in order to continue receiving recovery support, structure, and accountability, while re-engaging in work and/or independent living.
